How we protect your money:

1) Our property team does a professional appraisal on the property to ensure that the property is in healthy condition to retain its value.
2) We assess the borrower through the 5 C’s of Credit: Character, Capacity, Capital, Collateral, Conditions.
3) Our lawyers run a title search on the property
4) Our lawyers put you as a lean holder on the property

These 4 measures ensures that your investment is 100% secure against the value of the home. This guarentees that you will receive your interest earnings monthly, and receive your full investment principle immediately after the 1 year term expires.

WHY WE DO IT



The average resale house price in the GTA was $627,395 as of Sept 2015.

It is no wonder that most home owners in the GTA are living above their means. Statistics show the average family income at $76,219, while suggested family income to purchase and maintain such a home is $228,657. It is no wonder that many home owners are taking on more and more debt to maintain such a huge cost. Most eventually look towards high interest B-lenders once the conventional banks stop lending to them forcing them into a vicious cycle of high interest loan payments.

Our Mission

Is to rescue such home owners. Once you are caught in high interest payments - most of your money goes towards the interest, meaning you will never pay off the loan. Eventually, you will come to a point where the interest is so high, you will fall behind on your payments and the banks and other lenders will sell you property at below market value to recoup their funds. This leaves you and your family with nothing to show for after years and years of mortgage payments.

WHAT WE DO

We stop the bank's and other lenders' foreclosure process. We renovate your home and sell it for above market value. This ensures that you and your family walk away from the sale with enough money to start fresh. Moving forward, we work with you and your family to develop and execute a comprehensive financial plan to growth your wealth.
